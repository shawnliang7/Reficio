About Fast Fashion

The outfit cycle is moving faster than the planet can handle.

Fast fashion pushes constant newness: cheaper clothes, shorter trend cycles, and more pieces bought for fewer wears. Reficio turns that pressure into a design brief.

Our Solution

Reficio makes waste feel rare.

We source thrifted garments, inspect their quality, then redesign them with bleach illustration and coffee dye so every piece has a visible second life.

Sustainability Impact

Why upcycling matters.

Reusing a garment keeps existing fabric in motion and reduces demand for newly produced textiles. The circular economy starts when a piece is repaired, redesigned, and worn again.

SourceRedesignWearRepair

Estimated waste reduction

For every 20 thrifted garments redesigned, Reficio can divert roughly one small suitcase of clothing from donation overflow, landfill, or deadstock storage.

Behind The Process

From thrift rack to gallery piece.

  1. 01

    Source

    Find thrifted garments with quality fabric and redesign potential.

  2. 02

    Quality Check

    Inspect seams, stains, stretch, and fit before investing time into the piece.

  3. 03

    Bleach

    Sketch motifs, test exposure, and build linework slowly for control.

  4. 04

    Coffee Dye

    Add warm natural tones that make the finish softer and more organic.

  5. 05

    Final Product

    Rinse, dry, style, photograph, and present the second-life story.
